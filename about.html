Freeing Humans for Higher-Value Work

ROBOSPEC exists to solve one of the hardest problems in automation: bringing AI into real-world work. We build systems that learn from human skills and turn them into scalable robot execution - starting from single workstations and expanding across industries. Headquartered in Singapore, we operate at the intersection of global manufacturing, bridging real-world deployment with cutting-edge AI.

Our Vision

Traditional robots are like actors following fixed scripts - precise, but unable to adapt.
But real-world work is dynamic, variable, and deeply human.
ROBOSPEC is building a new class of robotic workers - systems that learn how humans work, and execute those skills reliably in changing environments.

Our goal is to make robots truly usable in real production.
Not through larger models alone, but through better execution - combining learning, engineering, and real-world deployment.
Step by step, workstation by workstation, we are turning human work into scalable machine capability.

We are building the foundation for AI-generated manufacturing
where robots are no longer programmed -but learned.

Built in Singapore, Designed for Global Deployment

Headquartered in Singapore, ROBOSPEC is positioned at a trusted and neutral intersection of advanced manufacturing, global capital, and cross-border innovation - building physical AI systems designed to scale across Asia and beyond.
