Our Vision

Traditional robots play the role of actors locked into pre-written scripts, capable only of rigid, singular operations. Yet, the real world is fraught with variables. Hence, the world urgently needs a solution that can truly learn human operational logic and possesses environmental generalization capabilities.

Our goal is to forge these "truly usable robotic workers." At the ROBOSPEC laboratory, from physical modeling and imitation learning network refinement to low-level agile arm control, we are making this a reality one step at a time.
